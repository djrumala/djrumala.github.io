My research focuses on <b>developing transferable Deep Learning models</b> to improve accuracy and efficiency of medical image <b>diagnosis</b>. Specifically, I aim to address <b>important questions</b> in the field such as: how to create <b>better generalized and robust</b> models, how to address <b>limited data, class imbalance, and variance</b>, how to increase <b>interpretability</b> of models, and <b>what types</b> of <b>medical image data</b> and <b>preprocessing techniques</b> are best suited for these models.
