My doctoral research focuses on developing transferable deep learning models for analyzing brain MR images to improve accuracy and efficiency of brain disease diagnosis. Specifically, I aim to address important questions in the field such as how to create better generalized and robust models, how to address limited data and variance, how to increase interpretability of models, and what types of brain MRI data and preprocessing techniques are best suited for these models
