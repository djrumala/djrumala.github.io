My research focuses on developing transferable Deep Learning models to improve accuracy and efficiency of medical image diagnosis. Specifically, I aim to address important questions in the field such as: how to create better generalized and robust models, how to address limited data, class imbalance, and variance, how to increase interpretability of models, and what types of medical image data and preprocessing techniques are best suited for these models
